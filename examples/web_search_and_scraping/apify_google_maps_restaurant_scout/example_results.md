Based on my search results for falafel places in Kadıköy, here are some great cheap and tasty options for you:

## **Best Cheap & Tasty Falafel Places in Kadıköy:**

### 1. **Falafella (falafel)** ⭐ 4.3/5
- **Price:** ₺1-200 (Very cheap!)
- **Address:** Caferağa, Moda Cd. No:53A, Kadıköy
- **Hours:** 11 AM - 2 AM (Late night on weekends!)
- **Why go:** The most affordable option with portions around 28 lira. Popular for falafel wraps (dürum). Many reviewers love the eggplant wrap and the generous toppings. Vegan options available!

### 2. **Nohut Falafel & Humus Restorant** ⭐ 4.8/5
- **Price:** ₺200-400
- **Address:** Osmanağa, Sakız Sk. No:22C, Kadıköy
- **Hours:** 12 PM - 10 PM
- **Why go:** Highest rated! Gluten-free and vegan options. Famous for their hummus and fresh ingredients. Reviewers rave about the quality and taste. Great for people with dietary restrictions.

### 3. **Jeni Falafel & Rolls** ⭐ 4.4/5
- **Price:** ₺200-400
- **Address:** Suadiye, Hamiyet Yüceses Sk. No:19, Kadıköy
- **Hours:** 12 PM - 9:30 PM
- **Why go:** Known for homemade taste and fresh ingredients. The owner is very friendly and welcoming. Great wraps and portion sizes.

### 4. **Tahin Restoran Caddebostan** ⭐ 4.6/5
- **Price:** ₺400-600
- **Address:** Caddebostan, Bağdat Cad. Çakır sok No 2, Kadıköy
- **Hours:** 11:30 AM - 10 PM
- **Why go:** Lebanese cuisine with excellent falafel and hummus. A bit pricier but highly rated for quality and unique flavors.

**My recommendation:** If you want the cheapest option, go to **Falafella**. For the best quality at a reasonable price, try **Nohut Falafel & Humus** — it has the highest ratings and people love their fresh, tasty falafel!
